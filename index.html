About Us

Our Vision

To be a leading innovation hub driving sustainable manufacturing, skills development, and community empowerment for national economic transformation.

Our Mission

To establish green manufacturing hubs, promote reverse engineering, foster skills enhancement, and empower communities through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships.

Main Goal

To support Uganda's transition from an agriculture-based economy to a diversified, industrialized, and innovation-led middle-income economy through inclusive, green, and locally driven development.

Strategic Plan 2025-2030

Phase 1: Institutional Setup & Pilot Development (2025)

Establish TAIG-GROUP legally, set up governance, identify pilot sites, conduct needs assessment, and forge initial partnerships.

Outcomes: Operational head office, 2-3 pilot centers, signed MOUs.

Phase 2: Program Development & Capacity Building (2026)

Develop customized curricula, recruit and train instructors, equip centers, and launch training programs.

Outcomes: Trained personnel, industry-aligned curricula, functional hubs.

Phase 3: Full Rollout & Innovation Integration (2027-2028)

Expand to additional regions, integrate R&D units, scale mentorship programs, and strengthen Cluster SACCOs.

Outcomes: National reach, scaled innovations, manufacturing startups.

Phase 4: Consolidation & Economic Impact (2029)

Evaluate performance, conduct impact assessment, institutionalize sustainability, and engage policy.

Outcomes: Increased skilled workforce, policy support, recognized contribution.
